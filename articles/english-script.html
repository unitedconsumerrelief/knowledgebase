(Training)

“Does this affect my credit score?

” (Only during the initial stage of the call)

“It won’t affect your credit score. This is just a soft inquiry with Equifax, so there’s no need to worry.

”1. Who are you? How do I know this is legitimate? Is this a scam?

I completely understand your concern, and I’m happy to clarify. We provide debt relief services through Cordoba Legal, a law firm specializing in debt resolution. This program has been offered in 49 states for over 10 years. It’s highly rated, with 5 out of 5 stars and accredited by the Better Business Bureau.

2. Is this a government program?

"This is completely legal and properly regulated. The program has been offered in 49 states for over 10 years and is highly rated for debt relief, with a 5 out of 5 stars and accreditation from the Better Business Bureau."

3. How does the program work?

"We specialize in debt negotiation and settlement. Our role is to work directly with banks to help you settle your debt for a lower amount, within a fixed timeframe, and most importantly, without interest. This way, you can get out of debt faster and improve your credit score."

4. Will this affect my credit score?

(Once you presented the plan) “The goal of this program is to improve your credit. During the first stage (the first 3-4 months where negotiations are settled), your score may drop a few points; however, from the sixth month onward, it starts to recover and increases as your debts are settled.

5. Do I pay you or the creditors directly?/How does this actually work?

“Of course. I’d be happy to explain. During the first month, is where negotiations are settled to establish agreements intended to eliminate the interest on your debt. Over the course of --months (depending on the plan you offered), we will be responsible for making the necessary payments to fully settle your debts.”

6. Can I make additional payments to pay off the debt sooner?

“Absolutely. Extra payments are always welcome. They help you accumulate settlement funds more quickly, allowing us to resolve your debts faster than initially presented.”

7. Do I still have to pay my creditors?

“No, once you're enrolled, we take over that responsibility. You will no longer need to worry about making any additional payments, as we will take care of these debts starting today."

8. Can I keep using my credit cards?

“We recommend not using the enrolled credit cards during the program to prevent the balances from increasing. However, if there are cards you wish to exclude, we can accommodate that. We typically recommend enrolling larger debts and excluding smaller ones.”

9. Can I apply for credit with the same bank again in the future?

“Yes, absolutely. Once your debt is settled, that account will show as paid, and you can apply for credit again in the future without issue. Settling your debts reflects positively on your credit worthiness.”

10. What if the bank starts calling me?

“Once you’re enrolled, we notify your creditors and begin communication on your behalf. If they call you, which is normal during the first stage of the negotiations, you can simply give them our number so we can maintain ongoing contact with both the creditors and you, and we’ll notify you each time we receive an offer or update.It’s very common for banks to reach out during the initial stage through insistent calls, emailsor text messages, this is with the goal to try to keep you as a client and continue earning interest. Our recommendation is to simply disregard those attempts, as we will be taking full responsibility for those accounts moving forward."

11. How do I know you’re actually settling my debts?

“Good question. You will be able to track the progress of your program in two ways.First, through your bank statements, where each settlement payment we make on your behalf will be reflected directly.Second, through your online portal. From the day you enroll, you’ll receive access to an online portal with a username and password, where you can monitor all updates to your program, including the payments being made.Additionally, you will have access to our customer service number, as well as my direct line, so you can contact us at any time with any questions or needs regarding your program."

12. Is this a debt consolidation?

"It's much better than consolidation. Consolidation will take you out of one debt and put you into another, often with interest. In our program, we negotiate the debt, eliminate interest, and settle it completely, thereby improving your credit score."

13. Is this bankruptcy?

Quite the opposite. The purpose of this program is to fix your credit and avoid bankruptcy, soyou can get out of debt as soon as possible and make your credit score increase."

14. What do you gain from this?

“Our service fees are already included in your monthly program payments. There are no hidden charges or extra payments — everything is fixed and interest-free, as outlined in your plan.”

Which means, what might take ___ years (depending on the total debt amount) topay through minimum payments could be resolved in just ___ months (depending on the length of the program we already offered). This is because, under our program, every payment is applied directly toward the principal. Plus, you can always make extra or additional payments to finish sooner.”

The program available in your state and for the type and amount of debt you have is this one, where the debt is frozen, interest is eliminated, and we help you improve your credit score."
